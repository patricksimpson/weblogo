### Supported Commands

#### Movement
* forward or fd
* back or bk
* right or rt
* left or lt
* setx
* sety

#### Looping
* repeat or rep

#### Drawing
* setpencolor or pc
* setbackground or bkg
* penup or pu
* pendown or pd
* clearscreen or cs

#### Turtle
* showturtle or st
* hideturtle or ht

#### Processing ####
* stop
